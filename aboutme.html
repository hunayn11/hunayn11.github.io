About Me

My name is Hunayn-Abbas. I am a second-year Digital Design student who enjoys playing and watching football. I have a strong passion for West Ham United and love the energy, loyalty, and atmosphere of match days.

Outside university, I work as a sports coach in a primary school, running breakfast clubs and after-school activities. I enjoy helping young children develop their skills, confidence, and passion for sports.

In my free time, I enjoy going to football matches, watching movies and TV shows, exploring new designs, and improving my digital skills. Football and creativity are the two things that drive me the most.
