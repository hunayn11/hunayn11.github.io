Football Basics

Football is a sport played between two teams of eleven players. The aim of the game is to score more goals than the opposing team.

How the Game Works

A football match lasts 90 minutes, split into two halves. Players mainly use their feet, except the goalkeeper.

Why Football Is Popular

Football is simple to understand, exciting to watch, and played all over the world by people of all ages.

Why I Support West Ham

West Ham United has a strong history, passionate supporters, and an incredible matchday atmosphere at the London Stadium.
