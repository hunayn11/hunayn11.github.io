Football Basics

Football is a simple and exciting sport played all around the world. Two teams of eleven players compete to score goals by kicking a ball into the opponent’s net.

The Aim of the Game

The aim of football is to score more goals than the other team. A match lasts 90 minutes, split into two halves of 45 minutes.

Basic Rules

Players cannot use their hands unless they are the goalkeeper. Fouls result in free kicks or penalties depending on where they occur.

Why West Ham?

West Ham United is known for its passionate fans, strong history, and famous motto: “The Academy of Football.”
